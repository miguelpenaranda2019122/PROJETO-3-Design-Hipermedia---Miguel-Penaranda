Moscow Mule

Ingredients

2 oz Vodka

2 oz Lime juice

8 oz Ginger Beer

Description

The Moscow Mule is a cocktail made up of vodka, lime juice, and ginger beer served in a copper mug. It was created in the 1940s in the United States and has become popular worldwide. It is a refreshing and fruity drink with a spicy kick from the ginger. It is often served with ice and a lime slice for garnish.
